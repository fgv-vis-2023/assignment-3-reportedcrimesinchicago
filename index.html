What is the most dangerous area in Chicago?

Using data taken directly from the Chicago Police Department, we computed crimes by Community Area in order to get a sense of the distribution of crimes across the city.

In addition to a breakdown by year, from 2014 to 2022, we used a word count to describe the crimes, trying to understand which words were most common in the police explanation of the crimes that occurred in Chicago. The font size represents the frequency of the description.
